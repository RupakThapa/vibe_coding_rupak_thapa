Intro Story

I didn't start as a designer who "just makes things look good."

I started as someone curious about how systems behave, how people think, and why tools either empower or frustrate us.

With a foundation in computer science and over four years of professional design experience, I now work at the intersection of UX, AI, and product thinking. Today, as a Master's student in User Experience Design in Germany, I explore how emerging AI tools can reduce friction, eliminate repetitive work, and create more human-centered digital products.
